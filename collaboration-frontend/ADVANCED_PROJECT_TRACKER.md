- **Budget Tracking**:

  - Budget utilization percentage
  - Visual progress bars with warning colors
- **Time Tracking**:
  - Estimated hours
  - Actual hours logged
  - Time utilization percentage
  - Overtime warnings

#### **Risks & Issues Tab**
Risk management and issue tracking:
- **Risks**:
  - Title and description
  - Severity levels (low, medium, high, critical)

  - Risk owner assignment

  - Title and description

  - Status (open, in-progress, resolved, closed)

  - Team member additions

### 3. **Smart Project Health Scoring**
Automatically calculated based on:
- Number of open issues (impacts health negatively)
- Critical risks (major health penalty)
- Days until due date vs progress
- Budget utilization
- Overall project status

Score ranges:
- 80-100%: Healthy (green)
- 60-79%: Warning (yellow)
- 0-59%: Critical (red)
